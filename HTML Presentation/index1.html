Co-occurrence Matrix

$$ \mathrm{Freq}_{i,j} = \dfrac{\text{Number of baskets where both station } i \text{ and } j \text{ appear}}{\text{Total_number of baskets}} = \dfrac{C_{i,j}}{N} $$

High-Frequency Co-occurrence Heatmap

Figure: The heatmap shows the normalized pairwise co-occurrence matrix (ranging from 0 to 1) across all stations. Darker pink squares indicate stronger associations between stations during peak hours. Only the lower triangle is displayed to reduce redundancy, helping highlight clusters of frequently co-traveled stations.
